Proprietary Software

SkyLord Eyes.
Autonomous Navigation.

Breaking free from GPS reliance. Our intelligent vision algorithm empowers machines to understand, map, and traverse complex, unpredictable environments in real-time.

The Brain Behind the Machine

Operating in GPS-denied environments like dense forests, urban canyons, or indoor industrial facilities requires more than basic telemetry. It requires true spatial awareness.

SkyLord Eyes uses advanced Visual-Inertial Odometry (VIO) and continuous Simultaneous Localization and Mapping (SLAM). It essentially allows our drones to "see" the world like humans do, mapping their surroundings frame-by-frame and predicting dynamic obstacles before they become a threat.

Core Algorithm Capabilities

Transforming raw pixel data into actionable flight physics.

Semantic Segmentation

Instantly categorizes millions of pixels into 'sky', 'building', 'human', or 'tree' to understand context.

Continuous SLAM

Builds high-resolution 3D point clouds of unmapped territories while simultaneously determining its own location within it.

Predictive Pathing

Calculates thousands of potential flight trajectories per second to actively avoid moving obstacles.
